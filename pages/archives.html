Sea Turtle Archives

The archive serves as a centralized digital repository of documented sea turtle nesting and hatchling events in Antique. It aims to preserve relevant information for research, conservation awareness, and educational use, while ensuring ethical protection of sensitive wildlife data.

Meet the Sea Turtles

Green Sea Turtle

Species:Chelonia mydas

General Location: Coastal Dalipe, Coastal Mojon, Malandog, & San Francisco Norte

Nesting Season: March – October

Source: LGU reports, PNA, CMPG-UPV, research papers

Year General Location Record Type Notes
2022 Coastal Dalipe San Jose Hatchling Release Assisted by LGU and volunteers
2023 Barangay Malaiba Nesting Activity Monitored by conservation group
2025 Coastal San Jose, Tibiao, & Hamtic Egg Discovery Reported in local news

Hawksbill Sea Turtle

Species:Eretmochelys imbricata

General Location: Coastal Dalipe, & Malaiba

Nesting Season: April – Novemeber

Source: PNA, CMS-ORS, CMPG-UPV, SEADEC

Year General Location Record Type Notes
2022 Coastal Dalipe Hatchling Release Community-assisted activity led by the Pawikan Center
2023 Barangay Malaiba Sea Turtle Sighting Cited in a study by UPV students
